About Simple Bistro

Simple Bistro started in 2016 with a simple idea: burgers done right. No frozen patties, no shortcuts — just real beef, flame-grilled on the spot, served with bold sauces and a side of good energy.

What began as a single spot in Summit, Addis Ababa, grew into a neighborhood favorite. Word spread fast. People kept coming back — not because we're fancy, but because we're real. Every burger is grilled fresh, every wing is tossed in house-made sauce, and every plate leaves the kitchen hot and honest.

Today we've got two branches — Summit and Bole — but the philosophy hasn't changed. Keep it simple, keep it fire, keep it craveable. That's Simple Bistro.
